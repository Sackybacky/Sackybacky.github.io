Strange contest launches in Wentzville

"A picture of a Ford plowing through snow."

The "Name-That-Plow" contest

What is it?

Wentzville has held their first ever "Name That Plow" contest this year, and it seems quite instresting.
As it goes without saying, it quite literally asks Wentzville residents to name the city's snow plows!

Who were the Winners?

Meet this year's winning plows:
#TagTheMayor — Christina Schaefer
CTRL+SALT+DELETE — Abigail Kelble
Snowbi Wan Kenobi — Chris Sipe
Scoop Dogg — Shara Grabenhorst

But Wentzville isn't the first...

This kind of contest have appeared in other states; this one being the first for Wentzville.
Some names that were produced by Minnesotans were We’re Off to See the Blizzard I Came, I Thaw, I Conquered Plow-a-bunga and Snowtorious B.I.G.
